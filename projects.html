MGT2010 Client Project

GYAF activities are largely volunteer-driven, many being youth. This creates challenges with brand and messaging consistency, which "creeps" over time and may stray from GYAF's original message and mission.

Project Goals: Propose communication/brand standards, including messaging guidelines, tone, voice, structure, etc. (aligned with WashU brand standards). Develop a website (structure and messaging) that reinforces the mission, converts visitors to the Intro to Flight program, engages youth consistently (encouraging return visits), and encourages and secures donors. The donor element is critical, but is dependent on the success of the website and social media strategies developed.
